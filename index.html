The Project

Our idea

Julia Child

Julia Child (1912-2004) is one of the most well-known and well-loved American television icons of the 20th century. Pioneer of a new television genre, the home-cooking show, French Cuisine evangelist, and unabashed feminist, she showed the country what it is to be a woman, to be a cook, and to be yourself.

Our Items

These 10 items represent Julia Child's culinary career and legacy throughout the last half of the 20th century.

The rise of Julia also saw the rise of television, mass media, second-wave feminism, and the Cold War. These 10 objects paint a broader and interconnected picture of twentieth-century America and Julia Child's place in it.
